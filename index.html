Elterwater Bird Experience - Glamping


Lake Elterwater is located on Lake Elterwater farm 50 minutes south of Blenheim, in Ward. The experience is unique - campers stay on site and are able to watch the natural birdlife on the lake during its various seasons from the Hiding, through an x8000 telescopes.

Lake Elterwater is also a working farm and campers are welcome to take part in day to day farming activities, depending on the season, on request. The farm is also 6km from Ward Beach, which features one of the best left hand surf breaks in New Zealand.
